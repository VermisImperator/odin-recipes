Chicken Salad

Simple and delicious, this healthy chicken salad recipe will help you get through your day. With a short prep time of only 5 minutes and cook time of 5 minutes, you can have a meal in a flash

The total calories for this specific chicken salad recipe are 779 calories, 63 grams of fat, 8 grams of carbs, and 44 grams of protein. Perfect for that workout bulking!

Instructions

  • 1/2 cup blanched silvered almonds
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of chopped, cooked chicken
  • 1 stalk of chopped celery

Ingredients

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Place the almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat and shake them frequently. They burn easy so be sure to cook evenly and stir them often.
  3. Take the mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper and stir them until evenly mixed in a medium sized bowl.
  4. Mix the chicken, almonds, and celery in the bowl with the sauce you created in the previous step.
