Chocolate Cake

No need to get an in-store chocolate cake with this recipe. Fulfill all your cravings with this amazing chocolate cake.

This low calorie cake only has 157 calories, 6 grams of fat, 26 grams of carbs, and 2 grams of protein.

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 and 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cups of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of boiling water
  1. STEP 1 Gather your ingredients.
  2. STEP 2 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two nine inch round pans.
  3. STEP 3 In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. STEP 4 Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Mix this for two minutes using the medium speed on a mixer.
  5. STEP 5 Stir in the boiling water last.
  6. STEP 6 Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  7. STEP 7 Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rake to finish cooling.
