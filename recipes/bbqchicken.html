BBQ Chicken

This homemade BBQ chicken sauce recipe for chicken wings that are not too sweet, not too tangy, and finishes in a crispy glaze!

This delicious meal has 452 calories, 13 grams of fat, 60 grams of carbs, and 26 grams of protein.

  • 1/2 cup blanched silvered almonds
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of chopped, cooked chicken
  • 1 stalk of chopped celery
  1. STEP 1 Gather ingredients
  2. STEP 2 Place the almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat and shake them frequently. They burn easy so be sure to cook evenly and stir them often.
  3. STEP 3 Take the mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper and stir them until evenly mixed in a medium sized bowl.
  4. STEP 4 Mix the chicken, almonds, and celery in the bowl with the sauce you created in the previous step.
