BBQ Chicken

This homemade BBQ chicken sauce recipe for chicken wings that are not too sweet, not too tangy, and finishes in a crispy glaze!

This delicious meal has 452 calories, 13 grams of fat, 60 grams of carbs, and 26 grams of protein.

  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of diced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic that is minced
  • 5 tablespoons of ketchup
  • 3 tablespoons of honey
  • 3 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pound of chicken wings
